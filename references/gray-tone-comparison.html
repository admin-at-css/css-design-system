Neutral Palette

The chosen gray system for the CSS Design System

Slate Gray - Selected

Following Refactoring UI principles, Slate Gray was chosen as the neutral palette because its subtle blue undertone harmonizes perfectly with our Deep Navy (#1b2a53) and Deep Sky (#356a9d) brand colors. This creates visual cohesion throughout the design system.

Project Overview

Slate gray creates a professional, trustworthy feel that harmonizes beautifully with the blue brand colors, providing excellent readability and visual comfort.

Text Hierarchy
Primary Headings and important content (slate-900)
Body Regular paragraph text for descriptions (slate-700)
Secondary Supporting text and labels (slate-600)
Muted Tertiary information and placeholders (slate-500)
Disabled Disabled text and inactive states (slate-400)

Brand Harmony

The subtle blue undertone in Slate creates visual cohesion with our Deep Navy and Deep Sky brand colors, ensuring a consistent, professional look.

Tailwind & shadcn/ui Native

Slate is the default neutral palette in Tailwind CSS and shadcn/ui, meaning excellent documentation, examples, and community support.

Excellent Readability

Slate provides strong contrast ratios for text, meeting WCAG 2.1 AA standards while maintaining a modern, clean appearance.
