Line Heights

6 line height options for different content

None 1
Single-line text only. Use for large display headings where text won't wrap.
Tight 1.25
Headlines and display text. Tighter spacing for large, bold text that needs visual impact.
Snug 1.375
Large text blocks. A balance between tight and normal, good for subheadings.
Normal 1.5
Body text for narrow columns. The default for most readable paragraph text.
Relaxed 1.625
Body text for medium columns. More breathing room for longer content.
Loose 2
Wide content areas. Maximum readability for very wide text blocks.
